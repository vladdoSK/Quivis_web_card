Purpose of the computer program Quivis

The computer program Quivis - is a server part of software code with access to it from web workplaces, which provides the ability to automatically collect LPR data from existing video cameras and other video analytics computer programs of the User Organization and build on this data:

  • selection of Incidents in accordance with the specified rules with notifications about their occurrence
  • analytics of traffic flows in the controlled territory of the Organization
    •

    Setting up and accessing Quivis

    The deployment and basic configuration of the Quivis computer program is created by the service company-authorized Integrator in cooperation with the Responsible Administrator from the User Organization, which is defined within the Supply Agreement. Not part of this User Guide.

    Users' access to the Quivis computer program is carried out through the web interface using a link determined by the Responsible Administrator from the User Organization. Access to the functionality and reports of the Quivis software computer program is granted only to authorized users in accordance with the authority granted to them by the Responsible Administrator. Enter your username and password in the web form that opens.

    General overview of the functionality of Quivis

    Map of cameras – displays the locations of the Organization's cameras, which are data sources for the software system.

    Vehicle search - an operational search for a vehicle on the controlled Territory of the Organization and/or its routes according to the criteria specified through this menu (vehicle number, beginning of the search period and end of the search period).

    Task – the list of sets of rules and conditions specified by the authorized User of the Organization, according to which individual Incidents are distinguished from the general flow of data from the Organization's cameras

    Incidents – access to the list of cases, the occurrence of which the authorized User of the software system would like to receive a notification. Each Incident is accompanied by information about which Task it was selected from the general flow of system events.

    Group of points "Operational Control of Zones"

    • Lists of numbers – сreation and editing of named lists of auto organization numbers that will be used when forming Control Tasks (examples: "Employees", "Management", "VIP guests", "With limited access" and so on).
    • Zones of control – named (recommended - with reference to the territories controlled by the Organization) groups of cameras that will be used in the formation of Tasks (examples: "perimeter", "Entrance A", "inner courtyard", "north campus" and so on).

    Reports – analysis of traffic on the territory controlled by the Organization for the selected period.

    Group of points Service

    • Report Non-working cameras

    Group of points Administration

    • Menu Users – creation of new users; editing their authority to access the functionality of the computer program.

    Available settings

    Map of cameras

    Map of cameras - works on the basis of Google map, the organization's cameras are displayed according to their geo-locations. The color of the camera marks is according to the map legend (upper right corner); depends on the type of systems for receiving information from them (configured according to p. 1) and the actual performance of the cameras in the current period.

    Vehicle search

    Vehicle search – the car number and the search period are entered in the corresponding fields of the form in the top line. if the date is not entered, the search is performed for the entire period. In the rows of the table with the search results - the date of fixation, the name of the camera and its coordinates, the name of the software system - data sources for the recognized number.

    Task

    Task – the list of active tasks of the Organization, which are currently created for Quivis by authorized users

    Incidents

    Incidents – a list of all cases, for all time, there is an opportunity to proceed to action, close incidents, view photos from the incident camera. Cameras analyze car number plates, and if the car number is being performed in tasks, an incident will be generated with the possibility of viewing a photo of the car.

    Icon in the "Photo" column - Demonstration of photos of the incident.

    List of numbers

    List of numbers – Creation and editing of named lists of car organization numbers, which will be used when forming Control Tasks (examples: "Employees", "Management", "VIP guests", "White list"; "With limited access" and so on).

    Zones of control

    Zones of control - named (recommended - with reference to the territories controlled by the Organization) groups of cameras that will be used in the formation of Tasks (examples: "perimeter", "Entrance A", "courtyard", "north campus" and so on) .

    Control tasks

    Control tasks – a list of sets of rules and conditions set by the authorized User of the Organization, according to which separate Incidents are distinguished from the general flow of data from the Organization's cameras. The system administrator has the ability to add, correct, delete tasks, change their status and type.

    Reports

    ЗвіReportsти – traffic analysis for the period. It is possible to choose any period. The report contains the number of active cameras, the intensity of road traffic on the territory controlled by the Organization with an assessment of its components: internal traffic; transit traffic on the territory and its components (grouping according to directions of entry and exit);

  • creating a map with camera locations by types of recognition mechanisms (Hik/Vezha) and by types of locations (T/V)
  • formation of a service report on cameras that did not provide data during the day
